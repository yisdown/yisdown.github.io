What I write about

Every write-up here is written with full methodology — I try to explain why something works, not just how. Categories include:

  • CTF Write-ups — Solutions to challenges from HackTheBox, TryHackMe, CTFTime events
  • Web Security — XSS, SQLi, SSRF, deserialization, and other web vulns
  • Binary Exploitation — Buffer overflows, ROP chains, format strings, heap exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering — Malware analysis, crackmes, firmware reversing
  • Cryptography — Breaking weak crypto implementations
